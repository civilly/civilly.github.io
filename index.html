Sian is available to work with you in preparing for and conducting weddings, funerals, naming ceremonies, commitment ceremonies and renewals of vows. She is also available to act as Master of Ceremonies for these or other events.

About Sian

Sian’s friends and clients describe her as warm, vivacious and engaging, with a fun and practical approach to life. An experienced and accomplished public speaker, Sian is confident and competent in addressing an intimate group of family and friends, or an audience of hundreds.

Initially qualified as a lawyer, Sian’s career has more recently been in human resources and management consulting. Her current full time occupation is as a loving mum!

She lives in Carnegie with her husband and two young children and is also fortunate enough to spend a lot of time in and around Porepunkah in the North East of Victoria, where she has family.

Sian’s interest in being a civil celebrant stems from her belief in the importance of relationships and commitment, her passion for friendships and social connection, and her appreciation for celebration.
